# CurtainAI Research Analysis

## Executive Summary
CurtainAI is an AI-powered mobile app (iOS/Android) that allows users to visualize curtains in their own rooms through photo upload and instant AI rendering. Developed by Metehan Kurucu, the app has generated over 14,000 designs with a 4.8 rating claim on the website, though App Store shows 3.7/5 with 16 ratings.

---

## Product Overview

### Core Value Proposition
**"Design your home with AI - see perfect curtains in your room instantly"**

The app solves a major pain point: the inability to visualize how curtains will look in your space before purchasing. This reduces returns, saves time, and eliminates guesswork in home decoration.

### Key Features
- **Instant AI Visualization**: Upload room photo → see curtains rendered in seconds
- **62 Colors, 12 Patterns**: Extensive fabric and design library
- **8 Style Categories**: Modern, Classic, Minimalist, Luxury, Bohemian, Industrial, Romantic, Smart
- **AI Color Matching**: Auto-suggests palettes based on existing room colors
- **Before/After Slider**: Compare original vs designed room
- **Text-to-Design**: Describe dream curtains without needing a photo
- **Community Sharing**: Share designs and get inspired by others
- **HomeGPT Assistant**: AI chat for personalized curtain advice
- **Premium AI Models**: Standard, Plus, Max quality tiers

### Technical Capabilities
- Auto-fits curtains to window size
- Analyzes room lighting, wall color, furniture style
- Multiple curtain types: drapes, blinds, sheers, blackout
- High-resolution render output
- Save and share functionality

## Market Position

### Competitive Landscape
- **General AI Interior Design**: VisualizeAI, Ideal House, Archit AI, Designly, Gaudi, Roomly
- **Curtain-Specific**: CurtainAI appears to be a niche leader in curtain-focused visualization
- **Differentiation**: Specialized depth (62 colors, 12 patterns, 8 styles) vs broad but shallow general tools

### Pricing Model
- **Freemium**: First design free + daily credits
- **Credit Packs**: $2.99 - $17.99 (no subscription)
- **Subscriptions**: Weekly $5.99, Monthly $14.99, Yearly $29.99
- **Premium Models**: Higher quality AI for paid tiers

4. **Multiple Revenue Streams**: Credits + subscriptions

### Weaknesses to Address
