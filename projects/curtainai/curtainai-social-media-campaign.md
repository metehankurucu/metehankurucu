## Visual Content Guidelines

### Brand Voice
- **Tone**: Inspiring yet approachable, confident but not arrogant
- **Language**: Simple, benefit-focused, emotionally resonant
- **Style**: Modern, clean, aspirational yet attainable

### Visual Style
- **Color Palette**: 
  - Primary: Soft neutrals (cream, beige, warm gray)
  - Accent: Deep teal, brass gold, charcoal
  - Pop: Terracotta, sage green
- **Typography**: Clean sans-serif (Montserrat, Inter)
- **Photo Style**: Natural lighting, aspirational but realistic spaces
- **Video Style**: Smooth transitions, satisfying reveals, upbeat music

### Before/After Rules
1. Always use slider format where possible
2. Show full room context (not just window)
3. Include lighting conditions (time of day)
4. Label clearly: "BEFORE" / "AFTER" or "ORIGINAL" / "AI DESIGN"
5. Add subtle branding (logo watermark)

### Text Overlay Best Practices
- Large, bold text in first frame
- Max 6 words per overlay
- High contrast (white text on dark background or vice versa)
- Emojis for personality (but sparingly)

## Campaign Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Low UGC Participation
**Mitigation**: 
- Increase incentives (more free credits)
- Create simpler share process (one-tap)
- Feature early participants prominently
- Run paid campaigns to seed initial content

### Risk 2: Negative Reviews/Comments
**Mitigation**:
- Respond within 24 hours with solution
- Proactive customer support
- Address subscription confusion with clearer UI
- Offer refunds immediately when requested

### Risk 3: Content Fatigue
**Mitigation**:
- Rotate content types weekly
- Introduce new features/styles regularly
- Partner with different influencers
- Tap into trending topics/sounds

### Risk 4: Ad Account Restrictions
**Mitigation**:
- Follow platform policies strictly
- Have backup accounts ready
- Diversify ad spend across platforms
- Use organic growth as safety net

### Risk 5: Influencer Underperformance
**Mitigation**:
- Vet engagement rates thoroughly upfront
- Provide clear content briefs
- Offer performance bonuses
- Have backup influencer list ready
