## üé® Creative Guidelines (Quick Reference)

### Visual Style
- **Before/After**: Always use slider format when possible
- **Colors**: Soft neutrals (cream, beige, warm gray) + teal/brass accents
- **Lighting**: Natural, warm, aspirational but realistic
- **Branding**: Subtle logo watermark, avoid over-branding

### Tone of Voice
- **Inspiring** yet approachable
- **Confident** but not arrogant
- **Simple** benefit-focused language
- **Emotionally resonant** (joy of perfect room)

### Content Pillars (% of content)
1. **Transformation Theater** (40%) - Before/afters that stop the scroll

3. **Community Canvas** (20%) - UGC and testimonials

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Common Pitfalls to Avoid

1. **Over-branding**: Let the transformation speak, not the logo
2. **Ignoring UGC**: User content is more authentic and performs better
3. **Inconsistent posting**: Algorithm punishes gaps, rewards consistency
4. **No engagement**: Comment back within 1 hour for best reach
5. **Generic content**: Specificity wins (show actual rooms, not stock photos)
6. **Forgetting CTAs**: Every post needs a clear next action
7. **Not testing**: Try 3-5 hooks before committing to one format
8. **Neglecting Pinterest**: It's a slow burn but compounds over time
